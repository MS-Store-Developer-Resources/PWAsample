Our Mission

Cycle Tracker is dedicated to providing a secure, private, and user-friendly way to track menstrual cycles. We believe that period tracking should be accessible to everyone while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and data security.

Privacy First

Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. All your period tracking data is stored locally in your browser, giving you complete control over your information.

Features
